About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to prepare emerging leaders and entrepreneurs through hands-on learning, competitive events, and community engagement across the fields of marketing, finance, hospitality, and management.

We empower Nova High students to develop leadership, career readiness, and teamwork through chapter meetings, service projects, and conferences at the district, state, and international levels.

What We Do

  • Weekly chapter meetings and skill-building workshops
  • Competition prep for role-plays, exams, and written events
  • Leadership development and officer training
  • Community service and school-wide initiatives

What is DECA?

DECA is an international association for high school and college students interested in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management. Members compete in role-plays and written projects, attend leadership conferences, and gain career-ready experiences.
